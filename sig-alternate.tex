\begin{abstract}

% Climate change
Social media became an instrument to support the climate change discourse.
% Campaigns and e-petitioning
In particular, Twitter enhances information dissemination for running various types of public campaigns.
Yet, e-petition dynamics of public environmental campaigns on social platforms still remain largely unexplored.
% Role of the petitions in the environmental campaigns
In this paper, we present an analysis of e-petitions that are promoted by the environmental campaigns on Twitter.
We further identify major social and content features of the tweets that correlate with the petition success.
% Petition success based on the campaign or Twitter activity
% Findings...
We have observed the differences between various petitions in their success/failure rate. We have found that petitions are mainly promoted by the campaigns with a high user engagement on Twitter, however, tweets and users outside of the campaign community correlate with a petition success as well.
Finally, we highlight general insights that typically lead to a petition success.

\end{abstract}
